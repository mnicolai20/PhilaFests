A Guide to Philadelphia's Spring and Summer Festivals

This site is your main tool for finding all of the information you will need to find, explore, and enjoy all of the festivals that Philadelphia offers.

A Few of the Best!

Flavors of the Ave: Features some of South Philly's best culinary offerings. Hosted on Passyunk Avenue

Italian Market Festival: Great food and drinks from all of the vendors in the Italian Market. Also, the famous grease pole climbing contest!

City Sips: Philadelphia's favorite happy hour with more than 90 bars and restaurants across Center City

Manayunk Streat Festival: Some of the best food trucks and vendors offer their best food, drinks, and shopping along Manayunk's historic Main Street.
